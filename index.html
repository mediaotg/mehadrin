Quality

Mehadrin's ice cream is produced in a state-of-the-art facility where only superior quality ingredients are used. The combination of advanced equipment and exclusive recipes creates the delicious, creamy texture and rich flavor you so love!

Innovation

Mehadrin is the only cholov yisroel producer of premium ice cream. Premium ice cream has less air and more butterfat than classic ice cream, making for a richer and creamier ice cream. Always looking to revolutioninze the kosher ice cream market, Mehadrin regularly adds new, thouroughly researched products to its extensive ice cream line.

Technique

Mehadrin learns from the best! From training at Penn State to Mr. Jim Biltekof, the first to introduce frozen yogurt in the US market, Mehadrin reaches out to the elite to develop a cholov yisroel formula - one that is on par, (or better!) than comparative brands.

Kosher

Under the strict rabbinical supervision of the Magriv HaRav, Rav Yeshia Katz, Mehardin’s ice cream is scrupulously kosher. Barring the use of DA cows, you can be sure our ice cream is as kosher as it is divine tasting.
